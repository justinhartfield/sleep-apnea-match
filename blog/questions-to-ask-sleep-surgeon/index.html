15 Questions to Ask Your Sleep Surgeon Before Surgery

Essential questions to ask your sleep apnea surgeon before your procedure, covering experience, risks, and recovery expectations.

Medically Reviewed by Dr. Igor I. Bussel, MD

Board-Certified Physician | UCI Gavin Herbert Eye Institute

Last reviewed and updated: January 2026

Before undergoing sleep apnea surgery, it is crucial to have a thorough discussion with your surgeon to ensure you are making an informed decision. A well-prepared list of questions can help you understand the procedure, its potential outcomes, and the associated risks. This conversation will also help you gauge the surgeon's experience and determine if they are the right fit for you. You should feel comfortable and confident in your surgeon's ability to address your concerns and provide the best possible care.

Your questions should cover several key areas, including your diagnosis, the recommended surgical procedure, expected outcomes, and the recovery process. It is also important to inquire about the surgeon's experience with the specific procedure being recommended. Do not hesitate to ask about the success rates of the surgery, both in general and for patients with a similar profile to yours. Understanding the potential risks and complications is equally important, as is knowing what to expect during the recovery period, including pain management and time off from work or daily activities.

Here is a list of questions to consider asking your sleep surgeon, categorized for your convenience:

**About the Surgeon and Their Experience:** * How long have you been performing this type of surgery? * What is your success rate with this procedure?

**About Your Diagnosis and Treatment Plan:** * What is my specific diagnosis, and what is causing my sleep apnea? * Why is surgery the best option for me compared to other treatments like CPAP or oral appliances? * What are the alternative treatment options available to me?

**About the Recommended Surgery:** * What specific surgical procedure do you recommend and why? * How will this procedure improve my sleep apnea and overall health? * What are the potential risks and complications associated with this surgery?

**About Recovery and Long-Term Outcomes:** * What can I expect during the recovery period? * How much time will I need to take off from work or other activities? * What are the long-term success rates and potential for recurrence?
