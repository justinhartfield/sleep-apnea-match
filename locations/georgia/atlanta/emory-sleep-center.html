Emory Sleep Center

Atlanta, Georgia

Overview

Inspire Certified

Sleep Medicine, Pulmonology, Neurology, Epilepsy Neurology

The Emory Sleep Center is a leading facility for the diagnosis and treatment of sleep disorders. They offer a comprehensive approach to sleep medicine, with a team of board-certified sleep specialists, nurse practitioners, and sleep technologists. They are also actively involved in research and education.

Procedures Offered

  • Jaw surgery
  • Mouth
  • throat and windpipe surgeries (tonsillectomy
  • uvulopalatopharyngoplasty)
  • Nasal surgeries
  • Nerve stimulation (Upper airway stimulation)
  • Tongue surgeries
  • Weight loss surgery
