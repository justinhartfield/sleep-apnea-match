Houston Methodist Sleep Center at Texas Medical Center

Houston, Texas

Overview

Inspire Certified

Sleep Surgery, Otolaryngology-Head and Neck Surgery, multidisciplinary approach to sleep medicine

The center is actively seeking a Division Chief of Sleep Surgery to develop a premier Sleep Surgery Center. The Houston Methodist Sleep Center is one of the only sleep centers in Houston accredited by the American Academy of Sleep Medicine (AASM).

Procedures Offered

  • Diagnostic sleep studies
  • Home sleep studies
  • "Inspire" titration
  • Multiple sleep latency testing (MSLT)
  • Maintenance of wakefulness testing (MWT)
  • Positive airway pressure (PAP) titration (both continuous and bi-level titrations)
  • Split-night sleep studies
  • Cephalometric Analysis
  • Overnight Sleep Study
  • Breathing Devices (CPAP)
