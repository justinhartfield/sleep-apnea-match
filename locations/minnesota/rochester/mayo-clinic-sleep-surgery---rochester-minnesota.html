Mayo Clinic Sleep Surgery - Rochester, Minnesota

Rochester, Minnesota

Overview

Inspire Certified

Sleep apnea surgery, soft tissue surgery, upper airway stimulation, orthognathic surgery

The Sleep Surgery Clinic at Mayo Clinic's campus in Minnesota provides specialized treatment for adults and children with obstructive sleep apnea who haven't found relief with other treatment options, such as continuous positive airway pressure (CPAP).

Procedures Offered

  • Soft tissue surgery
  • tonsillectomy
  • uvulopalatopharyngoplasty (UPPP)
  • Upper airway stimulation therapy (Inspire device)
  • Orthognathic surgery
  • maxillomandibular advancement (MMA) surgery

Key Surgeons

  • Stephen F. Bansberg, M.D., Semirra L. Bayan, M.D.

Recognition
