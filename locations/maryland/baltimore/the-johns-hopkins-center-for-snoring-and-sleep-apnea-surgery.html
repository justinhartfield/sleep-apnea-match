The Johns Hopkins Center for Snoring and Sleep Apnea Surgery

Baltimore, Maryland

Overview

Inspire Certified

Obstructive sleep apnea, snoring, hypoglossal nerve stimulation therapy

The center offers a multidisciplinary team and personalized treatment plans, including both surgical and non-surgical options. They also have a pediatric sleep care program.

Procedures Offered

  • Nasal Surgery (Septoplasty
  • Nasal turbinate reduction
  • Nasal valve reconstruction)
  • Palatal Surgery (Expansion Pharyngoplasty
  • Uvulopalatopharyngoplasty (UPPP)
  • Tonsillectomy
  • Palate radiofrequency
  • Palatal implantation)
  • Base of Tongue Surgery (Lingual tonsillectomy
  • Partial midline glossectomy
