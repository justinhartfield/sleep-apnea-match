University of Michigan Sleep Disorders Center

Ann Arbor, Michigan

Overview

Inspire Certified

Multidisciplinary approach with specialists from neurology, otolaryngology, pediatrics, psychiatry, and pulmonary medicine, behavioral sleep medicine program for chronic insomnia, sleep disorders in the context of other neurological conditions

The University of Michigan Sleep Disorders Center is one of the oldest, largest, and most widely recognized academic sleep programs in the U.S. They have a strong focus on research and a multidisciplinary approach to patient care.

Procedures Offered

  • Uvulopalatopharyngoplasty (UPPP)
  • Upper airway stimulation therapy
  • Radiofrequency volumetric tissue reduction (RFVTR)
  • Septoplasty and turbinate reduction
  • Genioglossus advancement
  • Hyoid suspension
  • Midline glossectomy and lingualplasty
  • Maxillomandibular osteotomy (MMO) and advancement (MMA)
  • Palatal implants
  • Weight loss surgery
