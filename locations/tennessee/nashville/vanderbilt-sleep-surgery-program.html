Vanderbilt Sleep Surgery Program

Nashville, Tennessee

Overview

Sleep Apnea, Snoring, Hypoglossal nerve stimulation (Inspire therapy)

First program in Tennessee to offer hypoglossal nerve stimulation (Inspire therapy).

Procedures Offered

  • Hypoglossal nerve stimulation (Inspire therapy)
  • Bilevel Positive Airway Pressure (BIPAP)
  • In-office snoring and sleep apnea treatments
  • Lifestyle changes
  • Nasal airway surgery
  • Oral (dental) appliances
  • Positional therapy

Key Surgeons

  • David T. Kent, MD (Director of Sleep Surgery, Director of Translational Research, Associate Professor), Holly Budnick, APRN

Recognition

Designated as an Excellence Program Care Team by Inspire Medical Systems.
