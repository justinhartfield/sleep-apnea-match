Sleep Apnea Surgery Procedures

Compare success rates, costs, and recovery times for the most common sleep apnea surgeries

UPPP

Most Common

Uvulopalatopharyngoplasty - removal of excess throat tissue to widen the airway.

Cost Range $5,000 - $15,000
Success Rate 40-60%
Recovery 2-4 weeks
View Cost Guide →
Newest Technology

Inspire Therapy

Implantable device that stimulates the hypoglossal nerve to keep airways open.

Cost Range $30,000 - $65,000
Success Rate 66-75%
Recovery 1-2 weeks
View Cost Guide →

MMA Surgery

Highest Success

Maxillomandibular advancement - jaw surgery to enlarge the airway.

Cost Range $40,000 - $100,000
Success Rate 85-95%
Recovery 4-6 weeks
View Cost Guide →

Septoplasty

Nasal

Correction of deviated septum to improve nasal airflow.

Cost Range $3,000 - $10,000
Success Rate 70-90%
Recovery 1-2 weeks
View Cost Guide →

Turbinate Reduction

Nasal

Reduction of nasal turbinates to improve airflow.

Cost Range $2,000 - $5,000
Success Rate 70-85%
Recovery 1 week
View Cost Guide →

Tonsillectomy

Pediatric #1

Surgical removal of tonsils, first-line treatment for children.

Cost Range $3,000 - $8,000
Success Rate 75-82%
Recovery 1-2 weeks
